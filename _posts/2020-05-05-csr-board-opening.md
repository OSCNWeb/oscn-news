Pursuant to 20 O.S. ยง1501, the State Board of Examiners of Certified Shorthand Reporters seeks a Court Reporter to fill a vacancy on the Board of Examiners effective July 1, 2020.  All members are appointed by the Chief Justice of the Supreme Court and serve a 5-year term.  

Eligibility:  Applicants shall be a certified shorthand reporter and a resident of the State of Oklahoma for at least five years, prior to appointment.  

To be considered for this vacancy, please send resume by June 5, 2020 to:
