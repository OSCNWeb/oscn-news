The Judicial Nominating Commission seeks applicants to fill a vacancy for the position of District Judge for the Third Judicial District, Office 1. This vacancy is created by the retirement of the Honorable Brad D. Leverett effective May 1, 2023.

Office 1 is an at-large position to be appointed from the Third Judicial District, including, Greer, Harmon, Jackson, Kiowa and Tillman Counties.  To be appointed to the office of District Judge for the Third Judicial District, Office 1, one must be a legal resident and registered voter of the district at the time (s)he takes the oath of office and assumes the duties of office. Additionally, prior to appointment, such appointee shall have had a minimum of four yearsâ€™ experience in Oklahoma as a licensed practicing attorney, a judge of a court of record, or both.

Applications must be submitted to the Chairman of the JNC no later than 5:00 p.m., Friday, April 14, 2023.  Applications may be mailed, hand delivered or delivered by third party commercial carrier.
