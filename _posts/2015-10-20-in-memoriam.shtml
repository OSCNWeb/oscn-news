The Honorable Thomas S. Crewson passed away at age 83 on October 14, 2015. Judge Crewson was a member of the Oklahoma Bar Association for over 50 years, serving as an attorney in private practice, a municipal court judge for the City of Tulsa, a Tulsa County juvenile court referee, a state District Court judge, and Chief Judge of the Tulsa County Juvenile Court where he presided for 14 years before retiring in 2002. He was an active member of the Juvenile Justice Oversight and Advisory Committee, which advises the Oklahoma Supreme Court about issues affecting children in the state courts.

The Oklahoma Supreme Court thanks Judge Crewson for devoting his career to the courts, the legal profession, and the people of Oklahoma. The District Court of Tulsa County expresses its appreciation for Judge Crewson’s love for kids and people in general and thanks him for his long and devoted service to the Tulsa courts.

Funeral services will be held at 1:00 pm on Friday, October 23, at the Boston Avenue United Methodist Church (1301 S. Boston Ave) in Tulsa.

For Judge Crewson’s full obituary and more information about services, please see the Tulsa World.
