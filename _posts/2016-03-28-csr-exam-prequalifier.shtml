The CSR Board of Examiners will be offering students an opportunity to take a pre-qualifier. This is in addition to the tests offered at the Oklahoma College of Court Reporting.

Date: Wednesday, April 13, 2016

Time:
6:45 p.m.
Registration from 6:00 to 6:15 p.m.
Warm-up from 6:15 to 6:45

Location:
D&R Reporting & Video
400 N. Walker Avenue, Suite 160
Oklahoma City, OK 73102

A 180 Literary and a 200 Q&A will be given.
Bring all equipment and supplies necessary, including a printer, paper, and extension cords. To sign up, call Melinda Wall at 405-627-3579 or email at melindawall@msn.com on or before 5:00 p.m., Friday, April 8, 2016.

As of March 8, 2014, any pre-qualifier passed shall be valid for a period of two years.
